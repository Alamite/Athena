# Apple Products Reference Guide

## Apple Inc.

Apple Inc. is a technology company that designs and manufactures consumer electronics, software, and online services. The company's primary product categories include smartphones, computers, tablets, wearables, audio devices, and digital services.

---

# iPhone

The iPhone is Apple's line of smartphones running the iOS operating system.

### Common Features

- Face ID facial recognition
- Retina and Super Retina displays
- Apple Silicon A-series processors
- 5G connectivity
- MagSafe wireless charging
- Dual and triple camera systems
- Ceramic Shield front glass
- Emergency SOS
- Satellite connectivity on supported models
- Apple Pay

### Storage Options

- 128 GB
- 256 GB
- 512 GB
- 1 TB (selected Pro models)

### Available Colors

Color options vary by model but commonly include:

- Black
- White
- Blue
- Pink
- Green
- Purple
- Natural Titanium
- Desert Titanium
- Space Black
